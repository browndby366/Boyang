Energy Star Score

The Energy Star Rating, also known as an energy efficiency score, is that a building earns using the United States Environmental Protection Agency online benchmarking tool, Energy Star Portfolio Manager, to compare building energy performance to similar buildings in similar climates. EPA, in conjunction with stakeholders, developed this 1-100 ENERGY STAR score as a screening tool, where the higher the score, the better the energy performance of the building.
